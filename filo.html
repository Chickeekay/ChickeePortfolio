The Challenge

Despite bringing in $32 billion worth of remittances in 2018 alone and despite 1/10th of the countries total population, there is no app yet that is specifically designed to serve the 10 million Filipinos overseas in managing their personal finances.

Back in 2016, I was chosen to join the Global Entrepreneurship Summit based on a business idea I submitted that is aimed to help migrant workers. This project is a part of that idea.
