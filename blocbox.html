The Challenge

Bombarded with information every minute of our lives, sorting and saving only the ones that actually matter to us has never been more essential. Blocbox aims to help us do just that.

MS Word, SD cards, and bookmarks were the most popular media storage people use. They still have their place. But as people become more agile, and the amount of data we consume keeps getting bigger, we need to create better storage.

Research



Survey Results

The survey mainly revolved around their current usage of their gadgets, their online browsing behaviors & the motivations for such behaviors.

I also asked about their backgrounds to see if there is a colleration between their occupation & their habits.  

The survey was done via Google Forms.

Their Goals:

1. Find an easy way to organized all of their online contents.

2. Seamless access to their files regardless of which device they use.

3. A sharing option for personal & professional contacts.

Their Frustrations:

1. The need ot use multiple apps & devices for saving content.

2. Bookmarks and saved files are not accessible in all types of devices.

3. Figuring out in which app or device they have saved a certain content.

Competitive Analysis

I compared 3 big names in the cloud saving and sharing industry namely: Evernote, MS OneNote and Box. Each has it’s own unique set of strengths & weaknesses, and opportunities & treats, both as an app & as a business.  

I don’t personally used any of these apps, so my competitive analysis data are based on various reviews I found online.
